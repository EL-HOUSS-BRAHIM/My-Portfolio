## üéØ Usage Guidelines

### Dark Theme (Default)
- **Hero Section**: Deep space background (#0a1428) with particle effects
- **Navigation**: Semi-transparent with cyan accents
- **Buttons**: Cyan primary, Magenta secondary, Pink accents
- **Cards**: Space blue (#1a2850) with neon borders
- **Links**: Electric cyan (#00d9ff) with magenta hover

### Light Theme
- **Hero Section**: Sky gradient with soft clouds
- **Navigation**: White with cyan glow effects
- **Buttons**: Gradient cyan-to-blue with elevated shadows
- **Cards**: White with colorful shadow glows
- **Links**: Deeper cyan (#0099cc) with bright hover

### Color Combinations
‚úÖ **Good Pairs:**
- Cyan + Magenta (primary + secondary)
- Cyan + Pink (electric contrast)
- Deep Blue + Cyan (depth with highlight)
- Orange + Purple (warm + cool)

‚ö†Ô∏è **Use Sparingly:**
- Pink + Orange (too warm together)

## üåà Color Philosophy

This palette captures the essence of time travel and cosmic energy:
- **Cyan/Blue**: Technology, space, time
- **Purple/Magenta**: Mystery, magic, dimension-hopping
- **Pink**: Energy, power, excitement
- **Orange**: Warmth, comfort in the vastness
- **Deep Blues**: The infinite void of space-time

The light theme maintains the same energy with a daytime sky perspective, showing how the same cosmic forces look different in daylight.
